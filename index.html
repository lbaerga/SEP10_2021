AP CSP at HSTAT

AP Computer Science Principles serves as a Senior Capstone for the Software Engineering Program at the High School of Telecommunication Arts & Technology.

Course Description

AP CSP is an AP-accrediting course. It is designed for students who are interested in Computer Science but who are new to it and may not have a strong background in Computer Science. The course is comprised of the basics of Computer Science, such as Abstraction, Algorithms, Programming, Computational Thinking, Data, The Internet, and Creativity.

Course Requirements

To apply for the course, students must be in good standing in the Software Engineering Program, alternatively, students may apply to take the course in their Senior Year. However, students applying who are not currently in the SEP program will need a Letter of Recommendation from one teacher, as well as to submit an essay detailing why they want to take the course and what they hope to get out of it.
